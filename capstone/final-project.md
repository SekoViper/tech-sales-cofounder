# Capstone Project: Become the Commercial Co-founder

## Objective

You will prove that you can operate as a serious commercial co-founder.

## Final project requirements

You must produce a complete go-to-market package.

## Part 1: Target customer segment

Define:

- Industry
- Buyer
- Users
- Main pain
- Current alternative
- Why now
- Ability to pay

## Part 2: Customer discovery evidence

Include at least 20 customer conversations.

For each:

- Business type
- Pain discovered
- Customer quote
- Urgency
- Buying process
- Next step

## Part 3: Positioning

Create:

- One-line pitch
- 30-second pitch
- 2-minute pitch
- WhatsApp pitch
- Proposal introduction

## Part 4: Demo strategy

Create:

- Demo script
- Demo path
- Expected objections
- Closing question
- Pilot offer

## Part 5: Pricing and ROI

Create:

- Pricing packages
- ROI assumptions
- Pilot pricing
- Discount rules
- Commercial model

## Part 6: Sales pipeline

Create:

- Lead list
- Pipeline stages
- Weekly targets
- Follow-up process
- CRM/spreadsheet view

## Part 7: Customer success plan

Create:

- Onboarding checklist
- First 30 days plan
- Success metrics
- Monthly review template

## Part 8: Final presentation

Prepare a 10-minute presentation answering:

1. Who are we selling to?
2. What problem are we solving?
3. Why is the pain urgent?
4. How does the product create value?
5. How will we sell it?
6. How will we price it?
7. How will we prove success?
8. What did customers teach us?

## Passing standard

You pass if you can defend your go-to-market plan with customer evidence, not assumptions.
